About FreshFruits

We believe everyone deserves access to fresh, pure, farm-sourced nutrition.

Our Story

FreshFruits was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to connect city dwellers with the freshest produce straight from local farms. We partner with over 50 verified organic farms across India, ensuring every fruit on our platform meets our strict freshness and purity standards.

Our AI-powered system monitors freshness in real-time, so you always get fruits at their nutritional peak. We believe that good health starts with good food — and good food starts at the farm.
